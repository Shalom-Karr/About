About Me

Hello, I'm Shalom Karr. As a web developer, my core objective is to build digital solutions that are both highly functional and intuitive for the end-user. I approach each project with a focus on meticulously translating complex user requirements into clean, secure, and scalable code.

My recent work on significant projects, including the Cleveland Marketplace and the GroupMe Copilot Image Bot, demonstrates a comprehensive approach to full-stack development. These endeavors have involved integrating robust backend systems using Supabase—leveraging its authentication, real-time capabilities, and database management—with dynamic and responsive frontend interfaces. I thrive on the analytical challenges inherent in software development and am committed to continuous learning to stay current with evolving technologies and best practices.

I operate on the principles of transparent communication, rigorous attention to detail, and a firm belief in technology's capacity to forge connections and streamline processes. Whether architecting a community-driven marketplace or a specialized automation tool, my ultimate aim is to deliver solutions that offer tangible quality and lasting value.
