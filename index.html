About Me

Hello! I'm Shalom, a developer dedicated to crafting web applications that are not only functional but also provide a great user experience. My focus is on understanding user needs and translating them into well-structured, secure, and maintainable code.

My recent work on projects like the **Cleveland Marketplace** and the **GroupMe Copilot Image Bot** reflects my commitment to full-stack development, integrating everything from user authentication and real-time features with Supabase to responsive frontend design. I enjoy the challenge of problem-solving and continuously learning new technologies to enhance my development toolkit.

I believe in clear communication, attention to detail, and the power of technology to connect people and simplify tasks. Whether it's a community marketplace or a specialized bot, my goal is to deliver quality and value.
